About Pharmacy Week

Pharmacy Week at UAB celebrates the critical contributions of pharmacists to healthcare. This year's theme, "Advancing Patient Care Through Innovation," highlights how pharmacists are transforming patient outcomes through cutting-edge research and clinical practices.

The event brings together students, faculty, professionals, and community members to share knowledge, network, and celebrate excellence in pharmacy practice.
